ABOUT

PT. Anugerah Bahari Suryatama is a logistic company that provides one-stop solutions for supply chain management with a commitment to ease logistics processes across various trades and industries.
Established in January 2014, PT. Anugerah Bahari Suryatama focuses on high-quality, accurate services and operates with skilled and experienced personnel.
The company serves several export and import sectors in commodities such as coconut, coffee, mangosteen, garlic, fish, furniture, and garments.
